Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026
This Privacy Policy explains how Kube Rush (“the App”) handles information when you use the mobile game published under the package com.dogusipeksac.kuberush.

1 Who we are

Kube Rush is a casual endless / level runner game developed by Doğuş İpeksaç. For privacy questions, contact ipeksac.dogus.19@gmail.com.

2 Information we collect

Depending on platform and settings, we may process:

  • Gameplay data on your device: coins/crystals, high score, settings, tutorial completion, equipped skins, and similar progress via local storage
  • Advertising identifiers: OS or ad SDK identifiers (for example Google Advertising ID / IDFA) when ads are shown
  • Diagnostics / crash data: if Firebase Crashlytics or similar tools are enabled — crash logs and device/app version information
  • Approximate analytics: aggregated usage events (if analytics are enabled), such as session starts; we do not ask for your real name, email, or phone number to play

Display names you choose in-game are stored for profile / leaderboard use and should not include personal data you do not wish to share.

3 How we use information

  • To run the game, save progress, and remember settings
  • To show advertisements (including rewarded ads) and measure ad performance
  • To improve stability (crash reports) and fix bugs
  • To grant promotional rewards such as one-time share bonuses stored only on device

4 Advertising

The App may use Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) and consent tooling (for example Google UMP) where required. Ad partners may use cookies or device identifiers to serve personalized or non-personalized ads according to your consent choices and regional law (including GDPR / CCPA where applicable).

You can often limit ad personalization in your device settings (Android Advertising ID / iOS App Tracking Transparency / Limit Ad Tracking). Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

5 Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. Data may be processed by service providers solely to operate the App, for example:

  • Google: Ads / Mobile Ads / Firebase
  • Apple / Google: as app store platforms

These parties act under their own privacy policies when they process data as independent controllers or processors.

6 Data retention

Local progress remains on your device until you clear app data, uninstall the App, or we provide an in-app reset. Crash / analytics retention follows the retention settings of the underlying provider.

7 Children

Kube Rush is a general audience hypercasual game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children for the purpose of selling data. If you believe a child has provided personal data inappropriately, contact us and we will delete it where feasible. Where required by law, ads may be served in a non-personalized manner.

8 International transfers

Service providers may process data in countries other than your own. Where required, appropriate safeguards are applied by those providers.

9 Your rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain processing, and to withdraw consent for personalized ads. To exercise rights related to data we control, email us. For advertising identifiers, use your device / OS privacy settings.

10 Security

We use reasonable technical measures (including local encrypted storage for profile data where implemented) to protect information on device. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

11 Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy where permitted by law.
