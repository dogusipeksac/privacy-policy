Privacy Policy

App Plaka Kontrol mobile application takes your privacy and data security seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our app, how we use and protect that information.

Important Notice: This application uses Google Generative AI service for plate analysis. Analyzed images and results may be temporarily sent to Google servers for processing.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Camera and Gallery Permissions

  • Photos: Access to vehicle plate photos you capture with camera or select from gallery for plate analysis
  • Temporary Processing: Photos are processed only during plate analysis and are not permanently stored
  • Local Storage: Analysis results may be temporarily stored on your device

1.2. Technical Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version for app performance optimization
  • App Usage: Crash reports and performance data (Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Analytics Data: App usage statistics (anonymized)

1.3. Advertising Data

  • Google Mobile Ads: Google Ads cookies for personalized advertising
  • Advertising ID: Your device's advertising ID (for ad personalization)
  • Location Data: For location-based ads (only if you permit)

2. How We Use Information

2.1. Plate Analysis

  • Analyze your uploaded plate photos using AI
  • Determine whether it's a standard plate or APP plate
  • Provide analysis results to you

2.2. Service Improvement

  • Monitor and optimize app performance
  • Detect and fix errors
  • Develop new features

2.3. Advertising

  • Show ads to provide free service
  • Monitor ad performance
  • Deliver relevant advertisements

3. Data Sharing

Third-Party Services: We share data with the following services:

  • Google Generative AI: Your images are temporarily processed for plate analysis
  • Google Mobile Ads: Anonymous data for ad personalization
  • Firebase Services: Technical data for Crashlytics and Analytics
  • Authorities: Sharing with relevant authorities when legally required

4. Data Retention Period

  • Photos: Deleted immediately after analysis completion (no permanent storage)
  • Analysis Results: May be temporarily stored on your device, deleted when app is cleared
  • Technical Data: Typically stored for 90 days to 1 year
  • Advertising Data: Stored according to Google Ads policies

5. Data Security

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transfer
  • Secure data processing on Google Cloud infrastructure
  • Regular security updates
  • Protection against unauthorized access

6. Your Rights

  • Data Access: Request information about your processed data
  • Data Correction: Request correction of incorrect data
  • Data Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Objection: Object to data processing
  • Complaint: Right to file a complaint with relevant authorities

7. Children's Privacy

This application is not designed for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you become aware that your child's data has been collected, please contact us.

8. International Data Transfer

Google services (AI, Ads, Firebase) may use servers located in the US and other countries. Your data may be transferred internationally in accordance with these services' policies.
