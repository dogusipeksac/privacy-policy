Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how our license plate recognition application ("App") handles information when you use it on your Android device.

Camera Usage

The App requires access to your device's camera solely to capture images of license plates for real-time analysis. No photos or images are saved, stored, uploaded, or transmitted to any server or third party. All image processing is performed locally on your device and the captured image is discarded immediately after analysis.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. The App does not retain license plate information, camera images, or any other user data at any point during or after use.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
